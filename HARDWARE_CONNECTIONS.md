## Wiring Instructions

### Keypad
- Connect the Keypad pins as follows:
  - Row 1 to GPIO 2
  - Row 2 to GPIO 3
  - Row 3 to GPIO 4
  - Row 4 to GPIO 5
  - Column 1 to GPIO 12
  - Column 2 to GPIO 13
  - Column 3 to GPIO 14
  - Column 4 to GPIO 15

### Servo Motor
- Connect the Servo Motor:
  - Signal pin to GPIO 18
  - Power to 5V
  - Ground to GND

### Buzzer
- Connect the Buzzer:
  - Positive lead to GPIO 19
  - Negative lead to GND

### LEDs
- Connect the LEDs:
  - Red LED anode to GPIO 23 and cathode to GND
  - Green LED anode to GPIO 22 and cathode to GND
  - Blue LED anode to GPIO 21 and cathode to GND

## Conclusion
Ensure that all connections are secure before powering on the ESP32.
